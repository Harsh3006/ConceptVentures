About Us

At Concept Infracon, we believe that each development has to be inclusive and beneficial for the community.

We deliver projects of the highest quality, with a customer centric approach. Keeping up with the ever changing trends of construction, the company remains committed to its core professional values. Concept Infracon was established in the year 2011 and is into the field of construction and EPC and power of infra projects and power plants. The companies verticals are well supported by its fluent and effective distribution network, providing a strong base for client support. The focus of each development carried out by Concept Infracon is to develop each site in harmony with nature. Commitment to environment is one of the principle values of the company.
