Market Size and Forecast

Whether entering a new market, preparing for a product launch, knowing the size of your market is critical when forecasting revenue.

So, four steps are followed in the total-market forecast which is define the market, divide total industry demand into its main components, Forecast the drivers of demand in each segment and project how they are likely to change, and conduct sensitivity analyses to understand the most critical assumptions and to gauge risks to the baseline forecast.

Impact Assesment

An impact assessment is a process for identifying, predicting, and evaluating the environmental, health, social, and economic impacts of development proposals before allowing them to proceed.

There are four key types of impact assessments which include global assessments, policy impact assessments, strategic environmental assessments, and environmental impact assessments.

Ad Evaluation

The measurement of the success of a planned program of advertising in terms of sales, increased product awareness, wider distribution, or other program objectives.

It is generally opined that much of advertising expenditure is wasteful. Proper evaluation would help in finding out whether it is wasteful and if so, how much of it is so.

Concept Testing

The process of getting an idea evaluated by your target audience before it becomes available to the public. It includes anything from product concepts to ad campaigns.

It is achieved by using an online survey. The survey needs to be designed to analyze how respondents feel about your concepts or ideas. The data collected using these surveys are then used to determine what customers prefer or reject your idea.
